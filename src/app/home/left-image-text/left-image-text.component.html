History
Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel High School, Khetiwadi(HS)

Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel High School Khetiwad, Anand managed by Charotar Education Society, Anand was established in 1964. At that time the teaching learning started in the Home science section. Principal shree Bhikhabhai Gulabbhai Desai started teaching voluntarily. The same institution was shifted to Khetiwadi campus in 1965. The students of the school offered their labour in the construction of the building. Principal shree Vitthhalbhai Jethabhai Patel started the unit of 5th to 11thstd. In the beginning 2(two) acres of land were spared for the school but later on Shree Kanaiyalal Munshi allotted 7.50 acres of land for the development of the school. There was only one class of each std. in the beginning. The result of the school was 70% in the very first year of the establishment.
