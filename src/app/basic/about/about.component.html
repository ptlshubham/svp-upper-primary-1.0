History

Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel High School, Khetiwadi(HS)

Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel High School Khetiwad, Anand managed by Charotar Education Society, Anand was established in 1964. At that time the teaching learning started in the Home science section. Principal shree Bhikhabhai Gulabbhai Desai started teaching voluntarily. The same institution was shifted to Khetiwadi campus in 1965. The students of the school offered their labour in the construction of the building. Principal shree Vitthhalbhai Jethabhai Patel started the unit of 5th to 11thstd. In the beginning 2(two) acres of land were spared for the school but later on Shree Kanaiyalal Munshi allotted 7.50 acres of land for the development of the school. There was only one class of each std. in the beginning. The result of the school was 70% in the very first year of the establishment.

Principal Shree Ramanbhai Navrangbhai Patel, Shree Ambalal Chotabhai Patel and Ochhavlal H. Upadhyay played pivotal role in the development of the school without any remuneration. The Agriculture University did not expect any funds and the school did not receive grant from government. To meet the expenditure, farming was done in the 5 acres of the land and that income was utilized for the development of the school. No financial aid was expected from the society. Today a number of students who studied in the school have secured high position in the country and abroad.

This school has established its own place at state level and national level. The brilliant students have secured good ranks In S.S.C. board examination. Prajapati Anuj Haribhai stood First in the state securing 96.86% of marks in S.S.C. board examination. In 2011 Sarvaiya Parth brought name and fame to the school by his outstanding performance.

The campus of the school has been well known as a green campus in the area.
